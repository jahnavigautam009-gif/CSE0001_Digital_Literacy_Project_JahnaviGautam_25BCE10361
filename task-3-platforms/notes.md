## Reflection

For Task 3, I explored two categories of digital platforms — a coding
practice platform and a cloud collaboration tool.

For Part A, I created an account on HackerRank and completed the
beginner-level "Solve Me First" challenge. HackerRank is widely used by
students and professionals to practise problem-solving and prepare for
technical interviews. Even completing a simple challenge gave me
confidence to explore more problems going forward.

For Part B, I created a five-question Digital Literacy Awareness Quiz
using Google Forms. The form includes multiple choice and short answer
questions covering UPI safety, cybercrime reporting, and responsible
social media use. The form is linked to a Google Sheet that automatically
collects and organises responses.

These tools will be extremely useful academically. HackerRank will help
me build coding skills consistently, while Google Workspace tools are
essential for collaborative assignments and group projects throughout
my degree.
