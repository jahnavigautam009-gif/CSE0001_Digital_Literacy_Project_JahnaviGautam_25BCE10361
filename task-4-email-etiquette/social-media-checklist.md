# Social Media Do's and Don'ts for College Students

---

## DO's

1. Keep your profile professional — use a clear name and appropriate profile picture
2. Think before you post — ask yourself if you would be comfortable with your professor or future employer seeing it
3. Use privacy settings — regularly review who can see your posts and personal information
4. Give credit — always tag or acknowledge the original creator when sharing someone else's content
5. Engage positively — support peers, share useful resources, and contribute to healthy discussions
6. Keep your college email separate from personal social media where possible
7. Report harmful or suspicious content using the platform's built-in reporting tools

---

## DON'Ts

1. Don't share personal information publicly — avoid posting your phone number, address, or ID details
2. Don't post or share offensive, hateful, or discriminatory content of any kind
3. Don't engage with or respond to cyberbullying — block and report instead
4. Don't share exam papers, answer keys, or any academic material without permission
5. Don't accept friend/follow requests from people you do not know personally
6. Don't post photos or videos of others without their consent
7. Don't fall for fake giveaways, surveys, or links asking for your login credentials
