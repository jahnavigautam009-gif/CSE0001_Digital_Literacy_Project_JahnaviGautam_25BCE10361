## Reflection

For Task 2, I set up my professional digital presence across three platforms:
GitHub, LinkedIn, and Kaggle. Each platform serves a distinct purpose in the
academic and professional world.

GitHub is a code hosting and version control platform widely used by developers
and students to showcase projects, collaborate on code, and contribute to
open-source communities. I have already used it to organise this Digital
Literacy Portfolio, which itself demonstrates practical GitHub skills.

LinkedIn is the world's largest professional networking platform. It is used
for job searching, internship applications, and connecting with industry
professionals and alumni. I filled in my Education section with my current
degree at VIT Bhopal University and my expected graduation year of 2029.

Kaggle is a platform for data science and machine learning enthusiasts. It
hosts datasets, competitions, and learning resources, making it ideal for
CSE students who want to explore artificial intelligence and analytics.

Over the next four years, I plan to use GitHub to document all my coding
projects, LinkedIn to build my professional network and apply for internships,
and Kaggle to participate in data science challenges and develop technical
skills beyond the classroom curriculum.
